Common questions

Straight answers before you decide

Why add another expense to the project?

I typically cost less than one change order. The contractor has done hundreds of jobs like yours. You have done one or two. That information gap is what you are paying to close.

How do I know you won't side with the contractor?

I have no financial relationship with any trade on your project. No referral fees. No subs I need to keep busy. My only interest is your outcome.

Can't a general contractor handle all of this?

A GC manages their own subcontractors, which means their loyalty runs to those relationships. I have no stake in who wins the job. That is a structural difference, not a matter of personality.

I already have a contractor picked. Is it too late?

No. Bring them. I'll verify their license, bond, and insurance, review the bid, and make sure the contract protects you. You don't have to start over to get oversight.
